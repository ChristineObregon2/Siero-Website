Siero's Restaurant Seating Chart

Welcome to our restaurant! Here's a breakdown of our seating arrangements:

1. Tables 1-9 and 12-13: These are our cozy four-seater tables, perfect for small groups or families looking to enjoy a meal together.
2. Tables 10-11 and 14-17: These are intimate two-seater tables, ideal for couples or individuals seeking a more private dining experience.
3. Tables 18-20: These are our one-seater tables, designed for solo diners who prefer their own space while enjoying our delicious cuisine.
4. Tables 21-24: Enjoy the comfort of our booth tables, offering a bit more privacy and space for groups of up to four people.
5. Table 25: Our centerpiece is a rounded eight-seater table, allowing larger groups to gather and share memorable moments while indulging in our culinary delights. In total, we offer seating for 25 guests across our diverse range of table options.

No matter the occasion, we have the perfect seating arrangement to accommodate your party and ensure a delightful dining experience for all.
